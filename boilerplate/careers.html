(A) Equal Opportunity Specialists

Agency : Department of Agriculture

Location : Washington DC

Experience :

  1. At least five (5) years of experience working as an attorney (current bar membership not required); or at least five (5) years of experience processing formal EEO complaints pursuant 29 C.F.R. § 1614 and a professional law degree (JD or LLB); and
  2. In addition to above at least three (3) years of experience working on Federal sector EEO matters; and
  3. In addition to above also at least one (1) year of experience reviewing ROIs for legal sufficiency, as identified in 29 C.F.R. § 1614, or two (2) years of experience utilizing ROIs to draft FADs; and
  4. At least two (2) years of Federal sector work experience

Please send your resume to staffing@attainx.com
