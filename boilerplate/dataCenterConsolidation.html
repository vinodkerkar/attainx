AttainX has Data center consolidation is the process of reducing the volume of physical IT assets through highly efficient and scalable technologies. Organizations leverage data center consolidation to reduce operating costs.

Today's competitive business environment, with increased focus on enterprise IT, most CIOs are done with optimizing the platform and applications layers in their IT setup. Now, more and more enterprise CIOs are exploring avenues to optimize their IT Infrastructure investments and setup, given the incremental costs of underperforming / heterogeneous data center assets.
