KNOW ATTANIX

AttainX Inc., established on 2008, headquartered in Lynchburg, Virginia is a WOSB/EDWOSB SBA certified Hubzone, certified 8(a), and ISO 9001:2008 certified small business having Contract Vehicle GSA IT Schedule 70. We specialize in Information Technology Solutions, our expertise is in Software Application Development, Cyber Security solutions, Big Data, Mobile Application development, Enterprise Service Desk solutions, IV&V, and ERP Implementation and Maintenance.

FLEXIBLE

ADVISORY BOARD

AttainX advisory board consists of former Federal and commercial C-Level executives. Our board meets every quarter and reviews the roadmap and strategies of AttainX. The advisory board provides guidance, and direction on future next steps for AttainX, offer thought leadership for products that AttainX is building on Big Data, Analytics, Mobility and Cloud.
