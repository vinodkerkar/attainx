Software As A Service

  • Software as a service (or SaaS) is the modern way of deploying and delivering applications using a browser with internet connection.
  • Installing, configuring and maintaining the software is delegated to the Cloud Provider.
  • All the complex tasks are owned and maintained by the provider thus giving the organization quality time to focus on the business needs.
  • We specialize in providing SaaS solutions in various Big Data technologies like: Apache Hadoop, MongoDB, various Amazon related Databases. We also undertake hosting other proprietary software on need basis.
