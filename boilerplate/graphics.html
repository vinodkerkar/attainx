AttainX Inc., provides Graphics and Media support applications for telework management, program outreach, payment auditing, loan officer training, congressional reporting, and much more. This includes developing graphics and multimedia for websites and presentations. AttainX as prime provides project management to manage staff to ensure timely delivery using project plans and ensures all work complies and aligns with Standards and Best Practices. We provide information, technical, and management briefings and presentations to the COR and to other staff as requested on all aspects of the work being performed. We conduct and document project management reviews for projects in various stages including project controls, analysis, implementation, and deployment.

We perform graphics design, printing, publications, multimedia, photography, video production and technical writing support. We use technologies such as Microsoft Office, Adobe Creative Suites including DreamWeaver, Acrobat InDesign, Photoshop and Illustrator, Corel Draw Graphics Suite, Quicktime, AutoCAD etc. We produce more than 5000 graphics products for local sites annually. We also provide computer-generated products and services for graphics requirements to include overhead transparencies, charts, posters, 2D and 3D display items, flyers, etc.

Our team is responsible for printing and publishing 1000+ posters across the state. We also print presentations for various seminars and demonstrations. Our team provides support in digital editing and media file conversion, CDs and DVDs as needed. We upload and manage shared network storage space for video and image files. We provide digital video recording and digital editing services. Video recording services often requires performance of services outside of core hours due to the scheduling of events.
