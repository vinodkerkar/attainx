AttainX provide systematic and proactive measures to

  1. Develop and implement appropriate, cost-effective internal control for results-oriented management.
  2. Assess the adequacy of internal control in Federal programs and operations.
  3. Assess and document internal control over financial reporting.
  4. Identify needed improvements.
  5. Take corresponding corrective action.
  6. Report annually on internal control through management assurance statements.

AttainX insures effectiveness and efficiency of operations, reliability of financial reporting, and compliance with applicable laws and regulations.

AttainX provides development and maintains of internal control activities that comply with the following standards as,

  • Control Environment
  • Risk Assessment
  • Control Activities
  • Information and Communications
  • Monitoring

AttainX provides appropriate level of documentation needed to support this assessment. Documentation we provide are appropriately detailed and organized and contain sufficient information to support management’s assertion. It also includes appropriate representations from officials and personnel responsible for monitoring, improving and assessing internal controls. Specific assessment and documentation requirements to support management’s assurance statement on internal control over financial reporting.
